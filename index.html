Why AI may miss details in a PDF

Limitations / document review
Common chatbot behavior
01Search relevant chunks

Keywords or semantic matches are pulled from the PDF.

↓
02Summarize fragments

Unretrieved pages, tables, figures, maps, and appendices may never enter context.

↓
03Sounds complete

The answer can look like a full read while relying on partial evidence.

Better analyst / agent workflow
01Read by section

Chapter, method, metrics, data source, limitations.

↓
02Check visual evidence

Tables, figures, appendix, performance measures, maps.

↓
03Cite page evidence

Every conclusion has page number, quote, or says not read.

Transportation reports need evidence discipline. For RIP, PROTECT, NCHRP, FHWA, and technical memos: require page checks, tables, figures, methodology, and appendix review.
Use this as the reliability explanation. AI often behaves like retrieval plus summary, not page-by-page reading. For transportation reports, insist on sections, page citations, tables, figures, appendix, methods, metrics, and limitations.
